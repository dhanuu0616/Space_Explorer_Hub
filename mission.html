Famous Space Missions

Explore some of the most important space missions in history.

Apollo 11 (1969)

Lanched on:July 16, 1969.
First humans on the Moon. Astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin landed in 1969.

Mars Rover Perseverance (2020)

Lanched on:July 30, 2020.
Exploring Mars for signs of past life and collecting samples.

Hubble Space Telescope (1990)

Lanched on:April 24, 1990.
Famous telescope that captured deep space images and galaxies.

Voyager 1 & 2 (1977)

Lanched on:1-September 5, 1977.
2-August 20, 1977.
Explored outer planets and now traveling in interstellar space.

James Webb Telescope (2021)

Lanched on:December 25, 2021.
Powerful telescope studying the early universe and distant galaxies.

Chandrayaan-3 (2023)

Lanched on: July 14, 2023.
India successfully landed near the Moon’s south pole.
