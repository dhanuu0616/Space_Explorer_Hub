🖤 Black Holes

A black hole is a mysterious object in space where gravity is so strong that
nothing-not even light can escape from it.It forms when massive stars collapse.

⚫ Key Features

🌀 Strong Gravity - Black holes pull everything towards them

🌌 Event Horizon - The boundary beyond which nothing can return

⚫ Singularity - The center point with infinite density

🌠 Invisible - They cannot be seen directly

🌟 Types of Black Holes

⭐ Stellar Black Holes - Formed from dying stars

🌌 Supermassive Black Holes - Found at the center of galaxies

🌀 Intermediate Black Holes - Medium-sized (rare)
